DOWNHILL

With a wide variety of routes attracting first time donwhill cyclists as well as the most veteraned in the sport, Åre has quickly become one of eurpoes most sought after bike park to visit. The bike park offers a beautiful scenery overlooking Åre lake and the litte qauint village of Åre as well as first class trails.

With 7 green paths and 11 blue paths, the bike park is more than accomodating if this is your first time trying the sport. Make your way down winding paths and get a taste of the addrenaline inducing sport that is Downhill. For the more seasoned bikers there are 11 red paths as well as 10 black paths for the really daring.

WHITE WATER RAFTING

Prepare to get soaked and have a blast along the way. Join seasoned guides as you make your way down some of northern swedens most scenic rivers. With a wide variety of tours to choose from, ranging from fun family tours to the most technical of streams for the truely adventurous. Eveyone id sure to find something that suits them.

In the video above, you can get a glimpse of the "family tour" offers. Make your way down the beautiful Ånnsjön and stop along the way for some well deserved lunch and swedish fika.

HIKING

For the people looking for a light workout accompanying stunning views and breathtaking nature, hiking in and around åre is for you. With it's many different peaks to scale, åre is sure to provide something for everyone.

Starting in Åre village, take the gondola all the way up to the top of Åre mountain and walk it's many trails down, taking in the views along with the fresh air. If you're looking to sleep out in nature, "skutan runt" is the trail for you. Enjoy a 27km long trail stretching all the way around Åre mountain.
